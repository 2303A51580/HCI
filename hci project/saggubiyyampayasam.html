Saggubiyyam Paayasam Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sago (sabudana)
  • 1 cup coconut milk (fresh or canned)
  • 1/2 cup jaggery (grated) or sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cashews
  • 2 tablespoons raisins
  • A pinch of salt

Preparation:

  1. Rinse the sago in water and soak it for about 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  3. Add the soaked sago to the boiling water. Cook on medium heat until the sago becomes transparent, stirring occasionally (about 10-15 minutes).
  4. Once the sago is cooked, add the grated jaggery (or sugar) and a pinch of salt. Stir until it dissolves completely.
  5. Lower the heat and add the coconut milk to the mixture. Stir well and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Do not boil after adding coconut milk to prevent curdling.
  6. In a small pan, heat ghee and fry the chopped cashews and raisins until golden. Add this to the payasam along with cardamom powder.
  7. Allow the payasam to cool slightly before serving. It can be served warm or chilled, as per your preference.
