Barfi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped pistachios (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped almonds (optional)
  • Silver leaf (varak) for decoration (optional)

Preparation:

  1. In a pan, heat the ghee over medium heat.
  2. Add the milk powder and condensed milk to the pan, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  3. Stir for 5-7 minutes until the mixture thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.
  4. Add sugar and cardamom powder. Stir until the sugar dissolves, and the mixture becomes a thick mass.
  5. Grease a flat tray with ghee and pour the mixture onto it.
  6. Spread the mixture evenly. Garnish with chopped pistachios and almonds.
  7. Let it cool completely. Cut into square or diamond-shaped pieces.
  8. Optionally, decorate with silver leaf (varak) for an authentic touch.
