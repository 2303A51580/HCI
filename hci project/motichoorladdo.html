Motichoor Laddoo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 teaspoon orange food color (optional)
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 tablespoon melon seeds or chopped cashews (optional)
  • 3-4 tablespoons ghee for frying
  • Few saffron strands (optional)
  • Silver leaf (varak) for decoration (optional)

Preparation:

  1. Mix besan, food color (optional), and water to make a smooth batter. The batter should be of pouring consistency.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan. Using a slotted spoon, pour the batter through the holes, making small boondi droplets. Fry them until lightly golden and crisp.
  3. In another pan, combine sugar and water. Cook until you get a one-string consistency syrup. Add cardamom powder and saffron strands.
  4. Add the fried boondis to the sugar syrup and mix well. Let it absorb the syrup.
  5. Once the mixture cools down, shape it into small laddoos with your hands.
  6. Garnish with chopped melon seeds or cashews, and optionally decorate with silver leaf.
