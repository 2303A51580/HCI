Rasam Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup toor dal (yellow lentils)
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind pulp
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • Few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • 3-4 cups water

Preparation:

  1. Pressure cook the toor dal until soft and mushy. Mash it well and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix the tamarind pulp with water and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat the oil or ghee. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  4. Add the crushed garlic, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© for a minute.
  5. Add turmeric powder, black pepper powder, coriander powder, and asafoetida. Stir well.
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  7. Pour the tamarind water and cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  8. Now, add the cooked and mashed toor dal to the pan. Mix well.
  9. Add water to adjust the consistency. Bring it to a boil and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  10. Add salt to taste and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  11. Serve the hot rasam with steamed rice or enjoy as a soup!
