Ingredients:

  • 1 cup moong dal (yellow lentils)
  • 2 cups chopped mixed vegetables (pumpkin, beans, carrots, or spinach)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • Few curry leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Preparation:

  1. Pressure cook the moong dal with a pinch of turmeric until soft. Set aside.
  2. In a pot, cook the mixed vegetables with a pinch of salt and a little water until they are soft but not mushy.
  3. In a pan, heat coconut oil. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  4. Add a pinch of asafoetida and stir well.
  5. Add the cooked dal and vegetables to the tempering. Mix well.
  6. Add the grated coconut and mix. Cook for another 5 minutes, adjusting the consistency with water as needed.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
