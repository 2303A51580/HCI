Vegetable Korma Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, potatoes)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • 1/4 cup coconut, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 cup yogurt (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Preparation:

  1. In a pan, heat oil or ghee. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add the grated ginger and minced garlic. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Stir in turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the mixed vegetables and green chilies. Stir to coat the vegetables with the spices.
  7. Cover and cook for about 10-15 minutes, adding a little water if needed, until the vegetables are tender.
  8. Mix in the grated coconut and yogurt (if using) and cook for another 5 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
