Pulihora Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon oil (preferably sesame oil)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons roasted peanuts
  • 1 teaspoon jaggery (optional, to balance the tanginess)

Preparation:

  1. Cook the rice and let it cool completely. It’s best to use leftover rice.
  2. In a pan, heat the oil on medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal, chana dal, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until the lentils turn golden brown.
  4. Add the tamarind paste, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for a couple of minutes until the raw smell of tamarind disappears. If using jaggery, add it now and mix well.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix well to coat the rice with the tamarind mixture. Ensure there are no lumps.
  6. Stir in the roasted peanuts for crunch. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  7. Serve hot with papad, pickle, or yogurt on the side.
